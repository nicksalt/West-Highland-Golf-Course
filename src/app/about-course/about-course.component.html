A Slice of Heaven Nestled in a Picturesque Setting on the West Side of Madoc.

Conveniently located off of Hwy #7 (at Atkinson Rd) and east of Hwy #62 (off of St Lawrence St. east). Being the half way point between Ottawa and Toronto many travellers stop for a game of golf and/or sit on the patio for a relaxing drink and a bite to eat.

We are a public golf course and offer 12 challenging holes. Tight fairways and bent grass are a pleasure to play for both the novice and the experienced golfer.

We are a licensed facility and offer short order food for our customers.

Also enjoy our home made specials each week.
