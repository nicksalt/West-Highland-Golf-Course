West Highland Golf Course

Experience the only 12 Hole Golf Course in Hastings & Prince Edward County, Ontario.

For those who feel nine holes isn't quite enough & 18 holes is too time consuming or expensive, come & experience golf at West Highland Golf Course.

Boasting a 3,250 yard, par 47. Affordable & time efficent. A friendly course for family, seniors, juniors, novice or the experienced golfers to walk or ride.

Conveniently located off of Hwy #7 (at Atkinson Rd) and east of Hwy #62 (off of St Lawrence St. east). Being the half way point between Ottawa and Toronto many travellers stop for a game of golf and/or sit on the patio for a relaxing drink and a bite to eat.

We are a licensed facility and offer short order food for our customers. Also enjoy our home made specials each week.
